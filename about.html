Who are we?

Defendza is a privately owned UK company, primarily into cyber security consulting , training services and security solutions. The company is backed by a team of highly skilled professionals with more than 10 years of experience working for the top information security employers in the past and hold various industry recognized vettings / certifications such as OSCP, CREST( CCT Infra & Web Apps), CISSP.

Defendza believes in delivering the best in order to protect customers risks and offer the best advice helpful for their business, to retain their trust and loyalty offered to us.
